About Tavern Viewer

Tavern Viewer is a simple Hearthstone Battlegrounds card gallery viewer built with HTMX.

To view the card gallery, click on the "Card Gallery" link in the navigation bar.

To view a specific card, click on the "View a Card" link and enter the card's name in the search bar.
